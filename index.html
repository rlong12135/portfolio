Approach

Design choices should carry their weight in the final build.

Structure first

The strongest pages feel inevitable. Layout, spacing, and reading order do most of the work before visual effects are added.

Intentional visuals

Good styling is not decoration. Typography, color, and contrast should reinforce tone, credibility, and ease of use.

Deployable by default

Clean repos, stable hosting, and frictionless publishing matter. A site that cannot ship cleanly is not finished.
